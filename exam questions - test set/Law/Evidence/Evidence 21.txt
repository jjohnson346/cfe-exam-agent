Section=Evidence

Stem=A statement, other than that made by the declarant while testifying at a trial or hearing, offered in evidence to prove the truth of the matter asserted is called ____________________________.

Options=Indirect evidence | Hearsay evidence | Circumstantial evidence | None of the above

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=According to Rule 801(c), "hearsay" is a statement, other than one made...at the trial or hearing, offered in evidence to prove the truth of the matter asserted."See page(s) 2.717 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
