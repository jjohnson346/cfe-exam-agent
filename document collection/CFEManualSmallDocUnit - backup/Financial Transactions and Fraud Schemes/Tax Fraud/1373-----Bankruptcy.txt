Bankruptcy
Taxes owed as a result of filing fraudulent federal income tax returns are not dischargeable in 
bankruptcy. [11, U.S.C., Sec. 523(a)(1)(c). In Re: Harris, 59 BR 545 (BC-DC Va. 1986); Rev. 
Rule 87-99, 1987-2 C.B. 291.]
