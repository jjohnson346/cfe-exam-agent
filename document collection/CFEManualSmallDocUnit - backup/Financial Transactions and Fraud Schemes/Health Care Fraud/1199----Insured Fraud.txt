Insured Fraud 
Insureds may attempt to perpetrate a fraud by submitting fictitious claims forms: 
• Multiple surgeries. 
• Multiple office visits. 
• Foreign claims — Patient supposedly goes out of the country and falls ill. 
• Noncovered dependents.
