Section=Organizational Crime

Stem=The Corporate Sentencing Guidelines provide several possible remedies that can be assessed against an organization that is found liable for an offense. Which of the following is NOT an option?

Options=Prison Sentences | Restitution | Fines | All of the above

CorrectResponse=0

Explanation=If an organization is found liable for an offense, the guidelines provide for four types of remedies: fines, restitution, remedial orders, and probation. Individual perpetrators may. however, face prison sentences.See page(s) 4.522-4.524 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
