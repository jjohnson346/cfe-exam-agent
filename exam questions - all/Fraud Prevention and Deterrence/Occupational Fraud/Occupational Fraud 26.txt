Section=Occupational Fraud

Stem=Criminologist Donald Cressey divided occupational crime offenders into three categories. Which of the following is one of those categories?

Options=Independent businessmen | Absconders | Long-term violators | All of the above are categories

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=For further analysis, Cressey divided the subjects into three groups: independent businessmen, long-term violators, and absconders. He discovered that each group had its own types of rationalizations.See page(s) 4.607-4.609 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
