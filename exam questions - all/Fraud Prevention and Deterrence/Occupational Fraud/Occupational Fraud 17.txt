Section=Occupational Fraud

Stem=According to the 2010 Report to the Nations, which of the following types of organizations suffered the largest median losses from occupational fraud?

Options=Public companies | Private companies | Governments | Not-for-profit organizations

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Frauds at the private companies cited in our survey caused the greatest median loss at S231,000. This is $30,000 greater than the median loss suffered by public companies and nearly two-and-a-half times the median loss that occurred at government agencies and non-profit organizations.See page(s) 4.631 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
