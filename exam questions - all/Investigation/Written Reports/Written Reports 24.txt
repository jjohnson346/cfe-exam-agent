Section=Written Reports

Stem=In writing reports of fraud examinations, the format used by different writers varies widely. But most reports contain basic documents. Which of the following is considered a basic reporting document?

Options=Transmittal letter | Exhibits | Memorandum | All of the above are basic reporting documents

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=Reporting formats vary widely. Some organizations, especially governmental investigative bodies, use report forms so that case information is provided in a consistent manner The basic reporting documents are:•	Memoranda•	Exhibits, documents, or enclosures•	Indexes•	Cover page•	Transmittal letterSee page(s) 3.916 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
