Stem=Theta, a Certified Fraud Examiner is conducting an investigation in which he believes assets have been illegally secreted offshore. Which federal agency is likely to be involved in offshore investigations?

Options=Drug Enforcement Administration | Department of State | Office of International Affairs | None of the above

CorrectResponse=2

Explanation=The U.S. Department of Justice, Office of International Affairs (OIA), in certain circumstances, may offer assistance to U.S. fraud examiners seeking to recover offshore assets. OIA is responsible for:•	Extradition requests•	Requests for foreign documentary evidence, bank and business records, and other official documents•	Interviews and depositions of foreign witnesses•	Assistance to obtain appearance of foreign witnesses in U.S. courts•	Foreign searches and seizures, compelled handwriting exemplars, and electronic surveillanceSee page(s) 3.851 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
