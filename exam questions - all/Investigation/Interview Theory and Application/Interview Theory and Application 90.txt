Stem=Showing sympathy is never a good idea in an interview.

Options=True | False

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Human beings need sympathetic responses from others. They like to share their joys, fears, successes, and failures. This need for understanding differs from the need for recognition that requires success and increased status.
