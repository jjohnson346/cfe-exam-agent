Section=Interview Theory and Application

Stem=In interview situations, closing questions serve several purposes. Which of the following is/are included in those purposes?

Options=Reconfirm facts | Obtain additional facts | Conclude the interview | All of the above are purposes

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=In routine, informational interviews, closing the interview on a positive note is a must. During interviews, closing questions are asked for the following purposes: to reconfirm facts, to gather additional facts, and to conclude the interview.See page(s) 3.234 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
