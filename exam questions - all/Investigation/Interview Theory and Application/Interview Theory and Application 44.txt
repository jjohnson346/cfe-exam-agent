Section=Interview Theory and Application

Stem=In interview situations, certain forces inhibit communication, while others facilitate communication. There are several facilitators of communication. Which of the following is NOT a facilitator of communication?

Options=Recognition | Anger | Catharsis | Fulfilling expectations

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Facilitators of communication are those social-psychological forces that make conversations, including interviews, easier to accomplish. These facilitators require a basic understanding of what motivates people. Facilitators include: fulfilling expectations, recognition, altruistic appeals, sympathetic understanding, new experience, catharsis, need for meaning, and extrinsic rewards.See page(s) 3.210-3.212 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
