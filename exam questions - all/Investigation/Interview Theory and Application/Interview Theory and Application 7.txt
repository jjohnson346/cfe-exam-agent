Explanation=Facilitators of communication are those socio-psychological forces that make conversations, including interviews, easier to accomplish. These facilitators require a basic understanding of what motivates people. The facilitators are: fulfilling expectations, recognition, altruistic appeals, sympathetic understanding, new experience, catharsis, need for meaning, and extrinsic rewards.See page(s) 3.210 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
