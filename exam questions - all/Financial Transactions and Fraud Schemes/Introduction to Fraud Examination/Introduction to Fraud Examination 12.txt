Section=Introduction to Fraud Examination

Stem=According to Corporate Fraud Handbook, an expense reimbursement scheme falls under what major category of Occupational Fraud?

Options=Corruption | Asset misappropriations | Fraudulent statementsX  | None of the above

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Corporate Fraud Handbook established three major categories for occupational fraud: Corruption, Asset Misappropriations and Fraudulent Statements. It then broke down each category into several distinct scheme types. The subcategories of asset misappropriation schemes are listed below:•	Skimming schemes•	Cash larceny schemes•	Check tampering schemes•	Register disbursement schemes•	Billing schemes•	Expense schemes•	Payroll schemesSee page(s) 45-46 in the Corporate Fraud Handbook
