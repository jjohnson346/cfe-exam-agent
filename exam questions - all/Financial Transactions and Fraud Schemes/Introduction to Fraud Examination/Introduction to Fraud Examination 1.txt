Section=Introduction to Fraud Examination

Stem=In the fraud theory approach to fraud examination, it is necessary that the fraud examiner perform ail of the following tasks EXCEPT:

Options=Analyze the available data | Refine and amend the hypothesis | Create a hypothesis | Report hypothesis to management

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=The fraud theory approach dictates that the fraud examiner: (1) analyze the available data, (2) create a hypothesis, (3) test the hypothesis, and (4) refine and amend the hypothesis. It is not necessary to record or report the theory. Indeed, this theory should not be shared with others. A theory is necessary in complex investigations. It is just that —a theory.
