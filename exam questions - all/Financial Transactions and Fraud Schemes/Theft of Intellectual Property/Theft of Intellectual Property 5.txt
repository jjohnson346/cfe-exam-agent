Explanation=Corporate spies may be listening in on your meetings. One way to prevent this is through the use of a "quiet room." A quiet room may cost anywhere from $15,000 to $200,000, but it may be justified based on the risk involved. A quiet room is acoustically shielded and radio-frequency shielded.
