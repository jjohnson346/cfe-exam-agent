Section=Bankruptcy Fraud

Stem=In bankruptcy court, the debtor may be which of the following?

Options=An individual | A partnership | A corporation | Any of the above

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=A debtor is defined in Title 11, U.S. Coder Section 101(13) as a person or municipality involved in a case under this title which has been commenced. A debtor may be an individual, partnership, or corporation.See page(s) 1.1303 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
