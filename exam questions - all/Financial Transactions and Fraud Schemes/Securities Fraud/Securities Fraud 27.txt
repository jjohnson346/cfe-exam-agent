Explanation=Unlike the Securities Act of 1933. the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 mainly deals with post-issuance trading. The Securities Exchange Act of 1934, v/hich gave birth to the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), requires the registration of securities brokers and dealers.
