Explanation=Section 806 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act. which is codified in Title 18, U.S.C., § 1514A, creates civil liability for companies that retaliate against whistleblowers. It should be noted that this provision does not provide universal whistleblower protection; it only protects employees of publicly traded companies.See page(s) 1.1509 in the Fraud Examiner’s Manual
