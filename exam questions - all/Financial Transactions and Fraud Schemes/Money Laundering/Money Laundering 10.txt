Explanation=Section 352 of the PATRIOT Act requires all financial institutions to establish anti-money laundering programs, which must include, at a minimum: The development of internal policies, procedures, and controls to prevent money laundering. The designation of a money laundering compliance officer. An ongoing training program for awareness of money laundering. An independent audit function to test the programs.
