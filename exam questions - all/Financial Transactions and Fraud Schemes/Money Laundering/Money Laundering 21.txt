Stem=Baker owns a U.S. corporation. Approximately 20 percent of his sales occur in Australia. To avoid paying taxes on these overseas funds, the money is sent directly from Australia to a bank account in the Cayman Islands. The overseas account is in Baker's name, not the corporation's, and he is a signatory on the account. The account balance is approximately S75.000. Which of the following statements is true?

Options=Baker is required to file a Currency Transaction Report | Baker is required to file a Report of Foreign Bank and Financial Accounts | Baker is not required to report anything until the account balance is over $100,000 | Baker is not required to report the funds in the account until he brings the money into the United States
