Stem=To conceal an asset misappropriation in the company's books, a fraudster can enter:

Explanation=Asset misappropriations generally are concealed in the books of account as either false debits or omitted credits. However, many misappropriations are not concealed at all, and they will be reflected in the books as an out-of-balance condition.See page(s) 58 in the Corporate Fraud Handbook
