Section=Financial Statement Fraud

Stem=Which of the following is the correct calculation of the quick ratio?

Options=(Cash + securities) / accounts payable | Current assets / current liabilities | (Cash + receivables) / current liabilities | (Cash + marketable securities + receivables) / current liabilities

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=Quick ratio = (cash + marketable securities + receivables) / current liabilitiesSee page(s) 1.338 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
