Section=Legal Rights of Employees

Stem=Which of the following is NOT an element of defamation?

Options=A highly offensive statement | Publication to one or more persons | A false statement of fact | Harm to the plaintiff s reputation

CorrectResponse=0

Explanation=The four elements of defamation are:•	A false statement of fact•	Tending to subject the person to whom it referred to ill will or disrepute•	Published to one or more persons•	Made without privilegeSee page(s) 2.414-2.415 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
