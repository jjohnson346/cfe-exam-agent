Section=Bribery and Corruption

Stem=Illicit funds may be tunneled through payroll by making phony salary payments to which of the following?

Options=Existing employees for an inflated amount | Former employees | Fictitious employees | All of the above

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=Illicit funds may be generated by funneling through phony salary payments to fictitious or former employees or by making extra payments to presently salaried employees who then either return them to the payer or pass them on to the recipient.See page(s) 1.726 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
