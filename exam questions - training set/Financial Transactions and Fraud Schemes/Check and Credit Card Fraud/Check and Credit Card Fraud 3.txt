Section=Check and Credit Card Fraud

Stem=The process whereby acid-based chemicals are used to erase amount and payee information on a check is called:

Options=Frying | Scanning | Acid washing | Check washing

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=Check washing is a type of check fraud that involves using acid-based chemicals to erase amount and payee information on a check. The check is allowed to dry, after which a new payee and a greater dollar amount are inscribed.
