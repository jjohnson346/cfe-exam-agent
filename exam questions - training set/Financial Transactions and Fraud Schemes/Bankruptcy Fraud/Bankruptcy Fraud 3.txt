Section=Bankruptcy Fraud

Stem=Which of the following is an acceptable defense to bankruptcy crime?

Options=Concealment of assets was not from all creditors | Creditors had actual knowledge of concealed assets | Concealed assets were returned to the estate | None of the above choices are acceptable

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=Defendants cannot use as a defense to charges the fact that creditors have actual knowledge of concealed assets or that the concealment was not from all creditors. They also cannot use as a defense the fact that they returned the estate's assets, though this may mitigate damages.
