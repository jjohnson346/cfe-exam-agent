Section=Cash Receipts Schemes

Stem=Which of the following is the most common type of occupational fraud?

Options=Fraudulent financial statements | Bribery | Asset misappropriation | Extortion

CorrectResponse=2

Explanation=Asset misappropriations are by far the most common of all occupational frauds. There are three major categories of asset misappropriation schemes: cash receipt schemes, fraudulent disbursements, and theft of inventory and other assets.See page(s) 1.401 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
