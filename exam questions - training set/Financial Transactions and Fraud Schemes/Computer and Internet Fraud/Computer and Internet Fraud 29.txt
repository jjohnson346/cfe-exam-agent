Section=Computer and Internet Fraud

Stem=Communications activity logs maintain what information?

Options=Date, time, and duration of access | Programs executed | Modifications to password access capability | Data files accessed

CorrectResponse=0

Explanation=Computer systems maintain a variety of history files or logs. Communications activity logs keep track of the following: user ID; terminal identifier; dial-in port identifier; date, time and duration; error messages; and equipment malfunctions.See page(s) 1.1844-1.1845 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
