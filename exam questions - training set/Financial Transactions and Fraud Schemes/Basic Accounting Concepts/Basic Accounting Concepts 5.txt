Section=Basic Accounting Concepts

Stem=All of the following statements are true, EXCEPT:

Options=Asset accounts are increased with debits | Revenue accounts are decreased with debits | Expense accounts are decreased with debits | Liability accounts are increased with credits

CorrectResponse=2

Explanation=Entries to the left side of an account are referred to as debits, and entries to the right side of accounts are referred to as credits. Asset and expense accounts are increased with debits and decreased with credits, while liabilities, owners' equity, and revenue accounts are increased with credits and decreased with debits.
