Section=Legal Rights of Employees

Stem=Which amendment to the U.S. Constitution provides that a person has the right to an attorney and to confront the witnesses against him?

Options=Fifth Amendment | First Amendment | Fourth Amendment | Sixth Amendment

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=The Fourth Amendment to the U.S. Constitution prohibits unreasonable searches and seizures. The Fifth Amendment provides that a person cannot be compelled to give information which might incriminate him. The Sixth Amendment provides that a person has the right to an attorney and to confront the witnesses against him.See page(s) 2.402-2.403 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
