Section=Criminal Prosecutions for Fraud

Stem=Due process requires that the jury in a criminal trial reflect a cross-section of the defendant's community.

Options=True | False

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=The jury that hears a criminal case is not required to reflect a cross-section of the community or contain individuals of the same race or age as the defendant.See page(s) 2.517 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
