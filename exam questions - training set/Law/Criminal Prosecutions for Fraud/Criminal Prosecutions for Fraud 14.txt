Section=Criminal Prosecutions for Fraud

Stem=In a federal criminal trial, the defendant has how many days to file a notice of appeal?

Options=60 | 7 | 30 | 10

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=The defendant in a federal criminal trial is advised of his or her right to appeal at the sentencing hearing. A notice of appeal must be filed within 10 days or the right is lost, absent a showing of excusable neglect.
