Section=Law Related to Fraud

Stem=Which of the following is considered "obstruction of justice" under federal law?

Options=Tampering with a witness | Destruction of documents | Obstruction of a federal audit | All of the above

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=Obstruction of justice as a federal violation is contained in Title 18, U.S. Code, Section 1503, et seq. Obstruction of justice punishes, among other things, document destruction; influencing a juror; theft or alteration of a record or process; obstruction relating to court orders; tampering with a witness, victim, or an informant; obstruction of a federal audit; and obstructing the examination of a financial institution.See page(s) 2.332 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
