Explanation=Congress, in the Comprehensive Crime Control Act of 1984, mandated the uniform sentencing guidelines. The act also established the United States Sentencing Commission (USSC) v/hich began studying sentences for individuals soon after the passage of the act It was widely held in Congress that there was a great disparity of penalties for similar crimes committed by individuals. After three years of study, the USSC announced Sentencing Guidelines for Individuals.
