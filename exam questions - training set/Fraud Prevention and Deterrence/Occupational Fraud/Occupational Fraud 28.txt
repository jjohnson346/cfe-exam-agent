Section=Occupational Fraud

Stem=According to the 2010 Report to the Nations, which of the following is the most common fraud detection method?

Options=Accident | Tip | External audit | Notified by law enforcement

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Respondents to our survey were asked to identify how the frauds were first discovered. Forty percent of the cases in our 2010 study were uncovered by a tip or complaint from an employee, customer, vendor, or other source, making it the most common detection method.See page(s) 4.628 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
