Section=Interview Theory and Application

Stem=In an interview, the respondent may withhold information because of a perceived threat to his self-esteem. This is called an ego threat. Which of the following is one of the three categories of ego threat?

Options=Loss of status | Repression | Disapproval | All of the above

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=The respondent in some cases might withhold information because of a perceived threat to his self-esteem. There are three broad categories of ego threats: repression, disapproval, and loss of status.See page(s) 3.207-3.208 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
