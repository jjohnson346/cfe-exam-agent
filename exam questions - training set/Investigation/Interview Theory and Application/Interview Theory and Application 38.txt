Stem=How many interviewers, in general, should be used on a potentially volatile interview?

Options=Two | One | Three | None of the above

CorrectResponse=0

Explanation=There should be two interviewers involved in potentially volatile situations. This procedure provides psychological strength for the interviewers. Additionally, the second person can serve as a witness in the event the interview turns bad.See page(s) 3.233 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
