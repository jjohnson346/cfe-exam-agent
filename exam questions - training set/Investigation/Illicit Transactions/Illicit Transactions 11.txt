Stem=The following question is representative of what type of sampling? "If I believe some kind of irregularity might exist in the records, what sample size will I have to audit to have assurance of finding at least one example?"

Options=Attribute sampling | Discovery sampling | Random sampling | Statistical Sampling

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Discovery sampling deals with the probability of discovering at least one error in a given sample size if the population error is a certain percentage. This is the best type of sampling for auditing for fraud because it is sampling until one occurrence is found.
