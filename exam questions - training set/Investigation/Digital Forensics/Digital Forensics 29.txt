Section=Digital Forensics

Stem=If you are seizing computer equipment for forensic analysis, it is generally not necessary to seize printers.

Options=True | False

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=In addition to using printer-spool files to recover previously printed documents, many printers have internal hard drives that may contain information relevant to a fraud examination. In general, any information sent to and stored by a printer is recoverable unless the printer has overwritten the data.See page(s) 3.706 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
