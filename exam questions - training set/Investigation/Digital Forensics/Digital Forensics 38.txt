Section=Digital Forensics

Stem=There are three types of situations in which computer evidence is generally discovered Which of the following is one of those situations?

Options=Computer is the repository of evidence | Computer is the target of the crime | Computer is the instrument of the crime | All of the above

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=There are three types of situations in which computer evidence is generally discovered: (1) computers as the target of crime. (2) computers as the instrument of crime, and (3) computers as the repository of evidence.See page(s) 3.701 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
