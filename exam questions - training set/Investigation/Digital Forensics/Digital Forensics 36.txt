Section=Digital Forensics

Stem=Often, it is necessary to examine printers as well as computers for data because printers may contain internal _____________________ that may contain information relevant to a fraud examination.

Options=Phone connections | Network connections | Hard drives | Cartridges

CorrectResponse=2

Explanation=Many printers have internal hard drives that may contain information relevant to a fraud examination. In general, any information sent to and stored by a printer is recoverable unless the printer has overwritten the data.See page(s) 3.706 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
