Section=Written Reports

Stem=At least two witnesses must sign a confession; otherwise, it cannot be admitted in court.

Options=True | False

CorrectResponse=1

Explanation=Witnesses are not required to make a signed statement valid; only one person attesting to the authenticity of the signature is required. This person normally is the interviewer. However, although there is no legal requirement, it is a good idea to have two people witness the signing of a statement.See page(s) 3.921 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
