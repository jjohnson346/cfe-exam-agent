Section=Written Reports

Stem=in the section of a signed statement where the suspect is making an admission of guilt, the following information should be included:

Options=The statement has been read | The contents of the statement are truthful | The statement was voluntary | All of the above

CorrectResponse=3

Explanation=The confessor must acknowledge that he read the statement and he should initial all the pages of the statement. The statement should also state specifically that it is true and that the confessor gave it voluntarily.See page(s) 3.919, 3.921 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
