Explanation=As you move data from one venue to another there is always a concern. One of the concerns is data validity. Is the data valid and pertinent to your investigation? The second concern is integrity. Is the valid data you have selected correct? A simple check or audit of the validity and integrity of the data should be the first step in data analysis.See page(s) 3.613 in the Fraud Examiner's Manual
